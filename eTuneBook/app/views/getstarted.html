Getting started


1. Get familiar with the abc tune format

eTuneBook uses the abc tune format which is defined at abcnotation.com. There are many sources of abc tunes and abc tunebooks out there. At session.org for example you can find thousands of irish session tunes in abc format.


2. Have a look at the eTuneBook Manual

All eTuneBook functions are explained under Info > Manual.


3. Choose from the Book-Menu

  • Book > New: Begin from scratch
  • Book > Import: Import a Tunebook from your local disc or from your Google Drive
  • Book > Irish Tunes - Martin Fleischmann: Load Martin's Tunebook from the server
